Chains and Fighting Tips:
1. Try using Dive Kick after Twirling Kick hits.
2. Teleport attack commands:
--Hold Forward+ Tap the Weak Punch and Weak Kick buttons simultaneously.
--Hold Back+ Tap the Weak Punch and Weak Kick buttons simultaneously.
--Hold Up+ Tap the Weak Punch and Weak Kick buttons simultaneously.
--Hold Down+ Tap the Weak Punch and Weak Kick buttons simultaneously, This cannot be chained with by other Teleport Attacks.
3. Escape from Tired/Dizzy faster by tapping P and K buttons rapidly, if you succesfully recover, you will regain a small amount of power.
4. Zangya's attacks do less damage after each successful hit in a combo. After 10 hits she will only do 20% damage per attack. Supers are not affected by this.
5. Use air dash wisely, excessive use causes energy loss. When out of power you may only use a single air dash before landing.
6. Ki Mines will bounce off the wall before hitting the ground if you are too close to the edge of the screen.
7. If no Ki Mines contact the wall, they will be spaced in such away that if the opponent touches the closest mine to Zangya, he will be juggled on the rest.
8. Ki Mines can only be performed when no other Ki Mines exist. The same goes with Bouncy Ball.
9. Bouncy Ball can only do a maximum of 150 damage, but it can severely hinder an opponent's offense.

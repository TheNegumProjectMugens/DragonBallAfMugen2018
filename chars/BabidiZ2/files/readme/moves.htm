Babidi has many system moves inspired or taken directly from various fighting games, such as Capcom VS SNK, Marvel VS Capcom or King of Fighters. You may already be familiar with most of these moves.


 Backward Dash :
This is a classic back dash, allowing Babidi to move far from the opponent pretty quickly. Can be performed in the air.

Forward Dash :
A dash forward much like Vegeta Z2's. Hold Forward in order to keep dashing Can be performed in the air.

 Power Charge :
This is the classic Power Charge, during which you gain power quickly, in order to use your Super moves.

Focus Force :
This move is a variation to Street Fighter 4's Focus Attack. Hold both buttons to charge the move, release them to perform it. Here are Focus Force' various features :
- Repels projectiles. You need to release the buttons at the right time to repel the projectile. Works on single non super projectiles.
- Chargeable for Hyper Armor (1 hit). You still lose life when hit though.
- Can be Dash Cancelled, during charge as well as during strike. This allows you to dash the moment you hit your opponent with Focus Force, and follow quickly with other attacks.



 Short Power Drain : + (while close to opponent)
Babidi grabs his opponent and absorbs some of their energy using his container, after that they fall backwards.

Magician's Backstab : + (while close to opponent)
Babidi will electrocute the oponent with his Dark Powers and then summon a floating bomb to send the enemy upwards.



Orb Launcher: +
Poison-type. Babidi summons an energy up from the ground, hitting an airborne enemy for juggling.

Tornado Whirl: +
Wind-type. A small whirlwind is summoned and it moves forward. It can be used as a shield of sorts. It'll do 4 hits before dissolving.

Rock Throw: +
Rock-type. A big rock is summoned from the air and it drops right down on top of the enemy, flooring them.

Magma Bulb: +
Fire-type. Another anti-air, this time Babidi launches a small fire bullet into the air where it'll detonate.

Lightning Strike: +
Shock-type. Babidi creates a small ball of energy which slides across the ground as long as you hold the B button. Once you release the button, a lightning shock will strike at the exact spot the ball was.

Ice Bullet: +
Ice-type. An ice sphere is shot forward, moving slowly. Once it hits the enemy, it freezes them in place temporarily.

 



Babidi has a bunch of moves that will work only against compatible characters, to give more of a fullgame "feeling".

Dash Collision :
Compatible against : Goku Z2, SSJ Goku Z2, Vegeta Z2, Majin Vegeta Z2, Gohan Z2, Awoken Ichigo by Storm-Ex, Alucard by Dope, Yellow Suika by Veanko (Bluestreak by Diepod & Jesuszilla).
Run against a running opponent, and the characters will grab each other and struggle. Smash the buttons as fast as possible, and the winner will throw his opponent.

Optional animations :
There are a few other optional animations which you can include in your characters to make them compatible with Babidi Z2 :
- Flip Knockout : When hit by EX Snap Kick. Opponent goes flying in the air, flipping backwards (anim 5937).
- Broken Back : When hit by Kaiyoken with Nappa finisher (anim 5946 & 5947).
- Cursed : When hit by Skull Curse, the enemy gets temporary turned into a cursed state (anims 19030 to 19038).
- Chou Kamehameha Fatality : GetHit state fading to black when killed by Chou Kamehameha (anim 5941 & 5942).

If you wish to add some compatibility with Babidi to some of your characters, please head to this section to follow the tutorials.

 



Configuration file :
In Babidi Z2's root folder, there is a config.txt file in which you can set some preferences :
- You can choose to use either solid or transparent FX.
- You can choose which projectile repel FX is displayed during Slash Chop.
- You can choose which Super Background to use when you kill your opponent with a Super move.

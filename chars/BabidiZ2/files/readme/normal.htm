Movelist of Babidi Z2



Moves with the [EX] sign have an EX variation, meaning they can be performed with 2P or 2K too. EX moves have special properties, making them stronger than their normal counterpart, but at the cost of 1/2 powerbar.

 Wizard's Forward Teleport : or or (air OK)
Babidi Teleports forward, making sure he gets out of harm's way or setting himself up to continue a combo.

Wizard's Backward Teleport : or or (air OK)
Babidi Teleports backward, making sure he gets out of harm's way or setting himself up to continue a combo.

Gravity Trap: or or
An energy puddle is spawned on the floor (close-by Babidi with X, far ahead with Z and inbetween with Y)
and it stays on that spot for 3 seconds before it fades away and is lost.
When the enemy touches the energy puddle (usually by walking on it),
it explodes and the enemy is launched high into the air, setting them up for a follow-up attack

Curse Skull : or or (in the air)
A demonic skull is spawned and shot downwards. It breaks apart on the ground.
However if it touches the enemy, they will be temporary cursed.
A timer will show up to indicate how long the curse will last.

Sticky Bomb : or or
Babidi throws a small energy orb forward in an arc. It breaks apart if it hits the ground.
However, if it touches the enemy, it will stick to them for about 3 seconds, making it imposible for the enemy to get rid of it.
After 3 seconds it will unstick. But if you repeat the QCF+K command before that time, the bomb will detonate and damage the enemy!

Floating Bomb : or or (air OK) [EX]
The wizard plants an energy ball in mid-air close-by him and it will remain in place for about 3-4 seconds.
When the enemy touches one of these orbs, they explode. Very nice zoning move.
You can plant up to 2 of these energy orbs on-screen at the same time.
The EX version will plant a powered bomb that on detonation it will automatically create an additional bomb.

Pui Pui Summon: Puncher : or or [EX]
Babidi summons his Majin-ized guard Pui Pui! Pui Pui teleports into the stage and immedeately starts a flurry of punches,
ending with a (slightly) stunning gut punch. Then he teleports out again. X spawns him very close to Babidi, right in front of him. Y a little farther and Z even farther ahead.
The EX version has Pui Pui appearing in his X spot, but upon hitting the enemy he'll do two more teleport-and-flurry punch rushes and the last hit will knock p2 into the air.



Pui Pui Summon: Blaster : or or
Pui Pui appears on-screen and immediately charges his sniper blast, then shoots the projectile straight ahead. It explodes on impact.

Yakon Summon: Dash Slasher : or or
The mighty demon of darkness, Yakon, is summoned by Babidi. Immediately, the entire screen is completely blacked-out.
Yakon's eyes light up, partially revealing him. Then he dashes back and forth the screen, slashing the enemy as he does.
The game is paused during Yakon's entrance (or rather, the enemy is frozen in place). Yakon's attacks can be blocked.

Dabura Summon: Hell Blitz : or or
An underworld demon ruler, the great Dabura, is summoned by Babidi, right above him. Dabura then fires four projectiles downward.
If he hits with one of the blasts, the enemy will be engulfed in fire for an additional hit.



Dabura Summon: Flying Hell Burner : or or
Once more the demon lord is summoned, this time he performs a firey overhead fly-by assault.
Babidi will automatically shield himself from the fireballs.



Super Enchanter : or or (air OK)
Bibidi Babidi Buu! Our Wizard lord shoots an energy orb around the stage, it will bounce from the walls.
If the ball hits the enemy, they will be trapped by the wizard's powerful chants and repeatedly hit by a variety of his elemental attacks.
